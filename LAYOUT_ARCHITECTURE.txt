KEY FORMULAS (All measurements in inches):

Title Placement:
  Title Top:    0.5"
  Title Height: max(0.7, (len(title) ÷ 50) × 0.45)
  Title Bottom: 0.5 + title_height

Subtitle Placement:
  Subtitle Top:    0.5 + title_height + 0.15  ← Now accounts for actual title
  Subtitle Height: max(0.5, (len(subtitle) ÷ 100) × 0.35)
  Subtitle Bottom: subtitle_top + subtitle_height

Content Placement:
  Content Top:    subtitle_bottom + 0.15     ← Now accounts for actual subtitle
  Content Height: 7.5 - content_top - 0.3    ← Dynamic, based on above
  Content Bottom: 7.5 - 0.3

OVERLAP PREVENTION: 0.15" Gaps

Between Title and Subtitle:  title_bottom + 0.15" = subtitle_top
Between Subtitle and Content: subtitle_bottom + 0.15" = content_top

Why 0.15" instead of 0.1"?
  - 0.1" is 9.6px at 96 DPI
  - PPT font rendering creates soft shadows/padding
  - 0.15" (14.4px) provides visual comfort
  - Prevents any possible overlap even with font hinting

Result: Professional presentation with clear visual hierarchy
